      Recovery Code

      If you do not currently have your recovery code securely stored somewhere, you can generate a new one. Recovery codes cannot be retrieved - they can only be created. You are responsible for securing and finding your recovery code in the event that you forget your password.

      Delete Account

      !!! DANGER ZONE !!!

      If you wish to delete your outsidely account, you may do so via a two step process to confirm your desire. The first time the Delete Account button is clicked it will provide a confirmation code. You must place the confirmation code in the text box and click Delete Account a second time to truly delete your account.

      Deletion of accounts is permanent and irreversible.
