    What is Outsidely?

    Outsidely is a rudimentary activity tracker for those who are more interested in just getting outside than they are in their training or stats.

    What is Outsidely not?

    Outsidely isn't and won't ever be a replacement for feature rich fitness trackers like Strava and Garmin Connect.

    Creating Activities

    Activities can be added manually, or GPX files can be uploaded. Almost all major devices and apps support exporting as GPX directly. Many only do through their websites and not their mobile apps. Strava, for example, can export GPX files once data is synchronized there.

    Unfortunately, companies like Garmin, Wahoo, Coros, and Suunto do not provide public API access for users and their data. This means that it isn't possible to automatically sync activities from devices.

    Technical Information

    The challenge at the outset was to utilize only free or dirt cheap serverless services on Microsoft Azure.

    • Azure Functions are used on a consumption basis for processing and APIs.
    • Azure Static Web Apps is used for the website.
    • Azure Table Storage is used for storing and retrieving attribute data.
    • Azure Blob Storage is used for storing and retrieving binary data.
